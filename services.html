Our Services

Wedding Planning

Weddings are a big deal ... And you are too! CJN Event Planning will help you find, plan and achieve your dream day! With this assistance of CJN, and their wedding coordination team, you will be able to focus on the excitement of your upcoming celebration and benefit from the team approach to personalized planning.

CJN will offer you their knowledge of venues and vendors in San Luis Obispo County in a professional and courteous manner and make sure your needs are met... and then some!

Whether you are local to the area or planning your dream destination wedding, you will be in good hands with CJN.

CJN offers three different price point packages that will meet your needs and pocketbook. No matter which package you choose, CJN offers its Wedding Standards List to you at no additional cost. These standards will get the preparation of your big day started right.

Venue Management

Do you have a winery, church, or a property with breathtaking views in San Luis Obispo County that you would like to host events at?

CJN is honored to have a special relationship with several local venues where they serve as the primary contact and event planner for the destination. CJN can oversee all administrative and advertising-oriented tasks for your setting, as well as day of event oversight, including preparation and cleanup.

    CJN oversees event management for the following locations:

  • Mountainbrook Church
  • Sunset Vineyard Estate
  • Changala Winery
  • Four Lanterns
  • Carriage Vineyards
